Safety Tips & Resources

General Safety
  • Always share your live location with a trusted contact when traveling.
  • Keep your phone fully charged before heading out.
  • Stay aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts.
  • Stick to well-populated and well-lit areas whenever possible.
  • Keep emergency numbers on speed dial.
  • Carry a personal safety alarm or whistle.
Night Time Safety
  • Avoid isolated areas and poorly lit streets after dark.
  • Consider traveling with a companion when possible.
  • Avoid wearing headphones so you can hear your surroundings.
  • Plan your route before leaving and inform someone.
  • Use well-known and busy routes even if slightly longer.
  • Keep your keys ready before reaching your door.
Public Transport Safety
  • Wait in well-lit and populated areas at stops/stations.
  • Sit near the driver or in women-reserved coaches when available.
  • Note the vehicle number before boarding a cab/auto.
  • Share trip details with family when using ride-sharing apps.
  • Avoid empty compartments, especially late at night.
Digital Safety
  • Keep safety apps installed (SafePath AI, emergency SOS).
  • Enable location sharing with trusted contacts.
  • Save emergency contacts in your phone's ICE section.
  • Learn how to quickly trigger your phone's SOS feature.
  • Report suspicious behavior through community platforms.
