Know Your Legal Rights

Pakistani laws protect women from harassment, violence, and discrimination. Know your rights to take action.

You CAN file FIR without a male guardian
Recording evidence is LEGAL for self-protection
Police MUST register your FIR — refusal is illegal
No time limit to report sexual harassment
Street Harassment & Eve Teasing
PPC Section 509
Insulting modesty of a woman — verbal abuse, gestures, catcalling
Punishment: Up to 3 years + fine
PPC Section 354-A
Assault or use of criminal force against woman — touching, groping
Punishment: Up to 2 years + fine
Punjab Protection Against Harassment Act 2010
Covers stalking, following, unwanted contact, repeated messaging
Punishment: Up to 3 years + Rs. 500,000 fine
Workplace Harassment
Protection Against Harassment at Workplace Act 2010
Every organization MUST have a harassment committee. Your employer is legally required to protect you.
Committee must act within 30 days
Your Rights:
  • File complaint with internal committee
  • If no committee exists, file with Ombudsperson
  • Your identity must be kept confidential
  • Employer cannot fire you for reporting
  • Appeal to Governor within 30 days if unsatisfied
Federal Ombudsperson
Phone: 051-9224835 | Email: registrar@fospah.gov.pk
Domestic Violence
Punjab Protection of Women Against Violence Act 2016
Covers physical, emotional, psychological, and economic abuse by family members
Protection order within 7 days
Your Rights:
  • Apply for protection order (court restrains abuser)
  • Right to residence — you CANNOT be thrown out
  • Right to monetary relief and maintenance
  • GPS tracking can be ordered for repeat abusers
  • Violation of protection order: 1 year imprisonment
Punjab Women Protection Authority
Helpline: 0800-22-444 | Punjab Safe Cities: 0800-00-786
Cyber Harassment & Online Abuse
PECA 2016 - Section 21
Sending obscene/threatening messages, fake profiles, non-consensual sharing of photos
Punishment: Up to 5 years + Rs. 10 million fine
PECA 2016 - Section 24
Cyber stalking — repeated unwanted contact, monitoring, threats online
Punishment: Up to 3 years + Rs. 1 million fine
How to Report:
  • FIA Cyber Crime Wing: 051-9106384
  • Report online: www.fia.gov.pk/ccw
  • NR3C helpline: 1991
  • Take screenshots BEFORE blocking
